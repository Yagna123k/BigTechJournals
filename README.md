# BigTechJournals 🚀

**A place where techies unite to inspire, guide, and motivate others to crack Big Tech**

## About Big Tech Journals

Big Tech Journals is a curated storytelling platform where real tech journeys are shared to help others grow.
It brings together experiences, learnings, mistakes, and insights from people who have already made it into Big Tech, startups, and top tech roles.

Every story is meant to inspire clarity, confidence, and direction.

> Learning from someone who has already walked the path is the fastest way to grow.

---

## Why Big Tech Journals Exists

Everyone has access to resources today.
The real challenge is knowing:

* What to focus on
* How to use resources effectively
* Which patterns actually work
* What mistakes to avoid

Most people do not need more content.
They need **guidance they can trust**.

Big Tech Journals solves this by sharing real journals from people who cracked Big Tech and top tech roles, so others can learn directly from proven journeys.

---

## What We Share

* Big Tech internship and full-time journeys
* Interview preparation strategies
* Mistakes, failures, and lessons learned
* Career growth stories
* Startup and tech experiences
* Actionable insights, not generic advice

New stories are featured regularly, including a dedicated weekly spotlight.

---

## How the Platform Works (MVP)

* Stories are **curated and published by admins**
* Anyone can **read, like, share, and comment** on stories
* Story submissions are done through a review form
* No direct publishing by users to maintain quality
* Focused on reading experience, engagement, and trust

---

## Tech Stack

This repository powers the Big Tech Journals platform.

**Frontend & Backend**

* Next.js

**Database**

* PostgreSQL
* Prisma ORM

**Auth**

* Clerk

**Deployment**

* Vercel

The stack is chosen to be SEO-first, scalable, and low-maintenance.
